My Destinations

Mahabalipuram

It is known especially for its rathas (temples in the form of chariots), mandapas (cave sanctuaries), giant open-air reliefs such as the famous 'Descent of the Ganges', and the temple of Rivage, with thousands of sculptures to the glory of Shiva.

LOCAION:Chennai,Tamilnadu

Marina Beach

Marina Beach, or simply the Marina, is a natural urban beach in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, along the Bay of Bengal. George in the north to Foreshore Estate in the south, a distance of 6.0 km, making it the second longest urban beach in the world.

LOCAION:Chennai,Tamilnadu

Vellore Fort

Vellore Fort is a large 16th-century fort situated in heart of the Vellore city, in the state of Tamil Nadu built by the Emperors of Vijayanagara. The fort was at one time the imperial capital of the Aravidu Dynasty of the Vijayanagara Empire

LOCAION:Vellore,Tamilnadu

Yelagiri Hill

Yelagiri is a hill station in the southeast Indian state of Tamil Nadu.the hilltop Velavan Temple is a colorful Murugan temple with views of the valley. The Jalagamparai Waterfalls crash into a swimming hole surrounded by trails and picnic spots.

LOCAION:Chennai,Tamilnadu
